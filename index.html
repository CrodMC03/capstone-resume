Executive Summary

Carlos Rodriguez is a results-driven Analytics Professional with a strong background in human capital analytics, predictive modeling, data governance, and business intelligence. With extensive experience supporting the Department of the Navy (DON), Carlos has played a pivotal role in optimizing workforce planning, data-driven decision-making, and process automation.

Recognizing the growing importance of web-based applications in data science and business intelligence, Carlos is currently expanding his skill set by attending a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. This intensive program provides hands-on experience with front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript (ES6), React.js, Node.js, Express.js, SQL, APIs, and authentication frameworks. This additional expertise enhances his ability to develop, deploy, and integrate interactive data-driven applications, further broadening his impact in data analytics and visualization.

Carlos's commitment to professional development is evident in his ongoing graduate studies in Data Science, where he has consistently excelled in courses focused on statistical modeling, data manipulation, and advanced analytics. He also holds key certifications, including the IBM Data Analyst Professional Certificate and Lean Six Sigma Green Belt, reinforcing his expertise in process improvement and data quality assurance.

Throughout his career, Carlos has demonstrated exceptional leadership, problem-solving, and project management abilities, successfully collaborating across teams, modernizing data systems, and ensuring data integrity for strategic workforce planning. His ability to bridge data science, business intelligence, and full stack web development makes him an asset to any organization seeking transformative insights and data-driven solutions.
